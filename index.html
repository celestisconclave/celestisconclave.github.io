About Us

Where Curiosity Meets Rigour

Celestis Conclave was founded on the belief that the most profound discoveries happen at the intersection of disciplines — where a mathematician's intuition meets an engineer's pragmatism, and a physicist's wonder meets a computer scientist's ingenuity.

We are a regional STEM club that brings together students from across disciplines to collaborate on ambitious projects, explore cutting-edge research, and share knowledge through our publication and events programme.
